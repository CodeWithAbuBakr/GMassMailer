Privacy Policy

GMassMailer - Gmail Campaign Automation

Last Updated: July 2025

1. Information We Collect

GMassMailer is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary for the extension to function:

  • Authentication Data: We use Google's OAuth2 to authenticate with Gmail. We do not store your Gmail password or access tokens on our servers.
  • Campaign Data: Your CSV contact lists and campaign settings are stored locally in your browser's storage and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the extension's performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Providing the email automation service
  • Authenticating with Gmail's API
  • Improving the extension's functionality
  • Providing customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • Local Storage: All campaign data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage APIs.
  • No Server Storage: We do not store your contact lists, email content, or campaign data on our servers.
  • Encrypted Communication: All communication with Gmail's API is encrypted using HTTPS.
  • Access Tokens: Gmail access tokens are stored securely in Chrome's local storage and are automatically refreshed by Google's OAuth system.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gmail API: For sending emails and authentication. Google's privacy policy applies to this service.
  • Google Chrome Web Store: For extension distribution and updates.

5. Data Retention

Data retention policies:

  • Local Data: Campaign data remains in your browser until you clear it or uninstall the extension.
  • Access Tokens: Gmail access tokens are managed by Google and automatically expire according to Google's policies.
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data is retained for up to 12 months for service improvement purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all locally stored data through the extension's interface.
  • Deletion: You can delete all stored data by clearing the extension's storage or uninstalling the extension.
  • Control: You can revoke Gmail access at any time through your Google Account settings.

7. Children's Privacy

GMassMailer is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the extension or our website.
